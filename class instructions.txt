BaseLevel
level=1
stamina=25
willpower=100
spellpower=3
agility=3
critical=3

These are the base stats every mob has that are added after the normal calculations are done. So, if you set a mob's level to 119, it will become 120 when it's in-game. This doesn't actually change the calculations done to determine its stats; everything still uses 119 when multiplying by level!

Some things to note.

Level is their level.
Stamina governs their final Vitality score, reached by dividing Stamina by 8. For example, if something has 800 Stamina, it will have 100 Vitality in-game.
Willpower is their Mana stat.
Spellpower is their Power stat, everybody knows how SD works.
Agility is their cooldown reduction, I think. Not actually sure how relevant this is.
Critical is their critical hit chance. Probably. Agility and Critical has always been an esoteric concept for me, okay?

A lot of mobs have their stats defined by their class which can be found in classes.txt, but some have their class set to Daemon which makes things a bit more complicated. You can find the formula for them below and then you just fill in the blanks with the information taking from daemon_stats.txt. 

stamina += (8 * addvit) * lvl
agility += (addagi * lvl)
critical += (addagi * lvl)
spellpower += (addmag * lvl)
willpower += (addmana * lvl)

If we take a look at a Lupindor, we'd see its class was set to Daemon. We'll just head over to daemon_stats.txt and find it in the list, the slot in the various addvit and addagi bits in there to find out what their stats will be once it's in-game.

Easy!
